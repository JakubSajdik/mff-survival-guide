Classes in English: For a course credit, you must score at least 50% on the written test toward the end of semester.

Active participation in classes and satisfactory performance in an oral examination may also be taken into account.

A written test for which the score was lower than 50%, or that has been missed due to absence,

can be (re)taken latest by the end of the exam period that follows the semester. There is no other provision for repeated attempts at obtaining a course credit.
